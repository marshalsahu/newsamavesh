About Us

Samavesh or the Devanagari meaning of “Confluence of ignited minds” is the Annual Techno-Management fest of Veer Surendra Sai University of Technology, Burla. Ever since its inception Samavesh has lived upto its name of being the largest technical gathering in the state. Samavesh witnesses huge gathering of technocrats, business moguls, industry experts, scientist and students who expressed keen interest in technology. ​

Science Fiction or popularly known as “Sci-Fi” is the central theme of this year’s fest which is testimony to the fact that what may be ignored as a fiction may be tomorrow’s biggest creation. To replicate its wonder is the very thought of this year’s fest and would prove exhilarating yet challenging for our next generation.

  • MakerFest Odisha

    We are greatly honored to host Maker Fest Odisha, in association with. Motwani Jadeja Family Foundation Maker Fest is the Indian continuation of Maker Faire hosted across America, Europe, Africa and Japan, among others. Maker Fest, which launched in Ahmedabad, India in 2014, now hosts Tens of thousands attendees a year. It is the amalgamation of festivals celebrating the innovators and an art show with all the booths for inventors and demonstrations and workshops for attendees. Technocrats, artists and hobbyist across the country are invited to present their contemporary applications of ideas using the vast ocean of art, science and technology. Also, it is non-commercial and free for the public to attend.
